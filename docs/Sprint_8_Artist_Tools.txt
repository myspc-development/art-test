### Sprint 8: Artist Tools & Content Management
**Duration:** 2 weeks

**Goal:** Provide artist-facing features to manage profiles, content, and events.

**Tasks:**
- Allow artists to edit profiles, bios, and portfolios.
- Implement event creation and scheduling.
  - Add timezone support and recurrence options.
- Add tagging, categorization, and featured content tools.
- Provide preview and publish workflows.
- Add artist dashboard widgets for content stats.
- Include inline previews and drag-drop upload support for media.

**Deliverables:**
- Editable artist profiles.
- Event and content management UI.
- Artist-centric content dashboards with media preview features.
